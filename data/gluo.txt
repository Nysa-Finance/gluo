# Gluo

**Gluo** is Nysa’s community-facing agent, designed to support users, partners, and contributors through Discord.

Gluo acts as a **community manager and first-line support layer**, helping users understand the protocol, stay up to date with official announcements, and navigate Nysa’s ecosystem efficiently.
